Bio

Maria Flora is a Post-Doctoral Fellow at the Department of Economics of University of Verona (Italy). Maria completed her Ph.D. at University of Padova (Italy) in 2019, under the supervision of Professor Tiziano Vargiolu . Her Ph.D. dissertation was ranked as excellent by world leading experts. During her Ph.D. experience, she has been a Visiting Research Scholar (in 2017-2018) at the Mathematical Institute , University of Oxford (UK).
